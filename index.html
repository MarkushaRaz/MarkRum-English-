A new dish in the dining room

Today, on December 32, the unimaginable happened. A new dish called "Khachapuri" has appeared in our favorite dining room. According to experts, this dish should change the economics of this school. Manufacturers say that with the money they will increase the production of manufactured products.




Price increase

Today, on December 32, there was a sharp rise in prices in the dining room. The chocolate bar "Forest in bears" suffered the most. A rise of over 245%. The rest is 230+%. The mathematics experts of our school believe that this is due to the liberalization of prices in our dining room by order of the director. After this event, a meeting took place at recess 2 in the dining room and the director's office. As a result, the director promised to withdraw the document at the next lesson, and lower prices to the previous ones until tomorrow.




The reformation of the headmaster

Today, on December 32, the first reforms of our director began. Recall: after the inauguration 2 months ago, our current director promised to bring our school to the ranks of the best, and for this he will carry out large-scale reforms. And today the first of them began. The first was a general exercise before classes, to improve physical fitness. Many people don't like it, but no one from the administration is taking action. We will wait for developments.
